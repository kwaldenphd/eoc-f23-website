Here is one of my favorite recipes for turkey burgers if you'd like to try it!

Yield: About 10-12 Turkey Burgers

Amount Ingredient
2 lbs Ground Turkey
1 large Red Bell Pepper
1/2 large Leek
1/2 cup Fresh Cilantro
1 cup Dinosaur Kale
1 tsp Pink Salt
1 tsp Ground Black Pepper
1 tsp Italian Seasoning
1/2 tsp Chili Powder
1/2 tsp Garlic Powder

Instructions: Prehead the grill to medium high heat. Blend the veggies in a food processor or dice small and combine all ingredients into a large mixing bowl. Form into about 10-12 patties and grease the grill grates with cooking spray. Cook on the grill for 3-4 minutes per side. Serve with burger buns and toppings of choice. Enjoy!

Side note: I love eating these with sweet potato buns.

Recipe for Sweet Potato Buns:

What youll need: Large and fat Japanese Sweet Potatoes and salt.

How to make: Preheat the oven to 425 degrees Farenheit. Wash and Scrub the sweet potato and dry with a paper towel. Cut into about 1/2 inch thick rounds and lay flat on a baking sheet. Sprinkle generously with salt and place in the oven for about 30-40 minutes. Sandwich a turkey burger in the middle and enjoy!!
