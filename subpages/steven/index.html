Nanjing Delicacies

Nanjing, a city in eastern China, boasts a rich culinary heritage with a variety of delightful delicacies. Here are some of the most notable Nanjing delicacies: 1. Nanjing Salted Duck. 2. Duck Blood and Vermicelli Soup. 3. Nanjing Roast Duck.

Nanjing Salted Duck Duck Blood and Vermicelli Soup Nanjing Roast Duck
This is a signature dish, where ducks are marinated in a special salt mixture, creating a flavorful and tender meat. A distinctive and ubiquitous Nanjing dish, this soup combines duck blood with vermicelli, offering a unique flavor experience. A close cousin to Beijing's famous Peking Duck, Nanjing Roast Duck is characterized by its crispy skin and succulent meat.
