Cliff Lake, Ontario

This is a site about my favorite place on Earth, Cliff Lake. Each summer, my family and I take a week-long fishing trip to this secluded destination in the woods of Nothern Ontario. The lake features gorgeous scenery and incredible fishing; our personal records can be viewed here. It is truly a place like no other and somewhere I'm proud to say I've visited. While in Ontario, we'll see eagles, moose, and bears, making the 17-hour drive well worth it. I hope you think this place is as beautiful as I do!
