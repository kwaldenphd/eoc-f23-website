Logline World Characters
Nepo Baby follows the disastrously untalented daughter of Hollywood royalty as she fails her way through every career in the industry — acting, modeling, writing, you name it — while her overworked team tries to keep her life from falling apart (so they can keep their jobs.) Nepo Baby takes place in LA, specifically the entertainment company Galaxy. Magenta "Gen" Jones: Nepo Baby
Nepo Baby’s dad is the CEO and Chairman of Galaxy, and thus allows her to attempt any job she likes in the company, even though she is universally bad at everything. Naomi Ismail: Gen's assistant
