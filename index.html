Humans (and Pets) of Elements of Computing I!

The Elements of Computing sequence combines hands-on introduction to the basic concepts and technologies of computing with critical discussion of the historical, social, and cultural dimensions of computing, data, and digital technology. The work of the course will include discussions of content that foregrounds the cultural, social, and historical dimensions of computing technologies along with exploration and foundational skill building with various computing tools and methods.
  • Elements I focuses on the core components of computers & their operation, including foundational programming concepts
So far, we've covered hardware, operating systems/interfaces, networks, and the World Wide Web. We're working on some concept maps to connect our work so far, and are just getting started in Python. In the meantime, we also built a timeline!
